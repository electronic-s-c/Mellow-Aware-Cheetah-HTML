FAQs

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How does the marketplace work? Subcontractors create a profile with their experience, education and accreditations. They set their location and time availability. Hirers can search the pool of available contractors and select the perfect one for the job.
How are subcontractors vetted? Subcontractors go through a rigorous vetting process that includes background checks, verification of qualifications, and reviews from previous clients.
How are payments handled? You pay a small recruitment fee securely through the platform. Then you handle payments to your subcontractor yourself as you usually would.
Is there a rating system for subcontractors? Yes, you can rate and leave feedback for subcontractors based on their performance on projects. This helps maintain quality standards on the platform.
